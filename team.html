03 — Board

Practitioners who watch what we do.

The board is the room where assumptions get tested. They've seen this movie before — that's why they're useful.
— Investment Committee · 2026

The Board of Advisors is composed of industry practitioners — portfolio managers, research analysts, and senior allocators drawn from Chapman alumni and the broader Orange County investment community.

The board's role is not to approve every trade. It is to ensure the fund operates with institutional discipline: that policies are followed, that risk is monitored, and that analysts are challenged the way they would be in a professional setting.

Board members include current and former professionals at firms like PIMCO, Capital Group, T. Rowe Price, MFS, and Pacific Life — the same firms many Janes alumni now work at.
