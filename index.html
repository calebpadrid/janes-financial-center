Four pillars of the analyst experience.

Gestalt · common region
i. Discipline
Process before outcome
Every position passes a written checklist: thesis, catalysts, valuation, risk, sizing. Outcomes vary; the process is the edge.
ii. Capital
Skin in the consequence
Live AUM means losses are real and discussions are honest. There is no learning substitute for managing money.

Built across class years
Sophomores shadow seniors. Knowledge transfer is structural — onboarding decks, recorded pitches, mentor pairings.
iv. Network
A board that opens doors
Alumni at PIMCO, Capital Group, T. Rowe, MFS, Pacific Life. The Janes network is the placement engine.
