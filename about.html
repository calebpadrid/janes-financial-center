01 — Mandate

The investment policy.

We don't run a paper portfolio. Every position is a thesis someone defended in front of the board.
— Portfolio Manager's Note · 2026

The Janes Financial Center operates the Student-Managed Investment Fund as a structured equity and fixed income mandate benchmarked against the S&P 500 with a fixed income overlay. Analysts work in sector pods, pitch named positions, and own the P&L of what they buy.

Coverage spans five teams: Macro & Fixed Income, Consumer & Healthcare, Industrials & Energy, Technology, and Financials. Each pod publishes a quarterly outlook, presents stock pitches at twice-monthly meetings, and is held to a written investment policy statement reviewed by the board of advisors.

What distinguishes Janes from a finance club is the accountability infrastructure — risk limits, position sizing rules, sell discipline, and a board that asks the questions a real allocator would ask.
